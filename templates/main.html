Introducing Farm Assist

The agriculture chatbot powered by Dialogflow is a virtual assistant that uses AI to answer farmers' queries on various agricultural topics. It provides real-time information and advice on crop cultivation, pest control, soil health, and more. Empowering farmers with accurate and timely insights, it enhances agricultural practices and productivity.

What is Farm Assist?

Our agritech web app features an AI chatbot that provides farmers with weather forecast information. By accessing real-time data and insights, farmers can make more informed decisions about when to plant, harvest, and manage their crops. Our easy-to-use application is designed to improve yields and help farmers succeed.

Weather forecast

To help with real-time updates on weather conditions in their area, ensuring that they have the most up-to-date information available.

Fertilizer calculator

Fertilizer amount calculator to determine the optimal amount of fertilizer needed for their fields.

Fertilizer predictor

Fertilizer predictor is a tool designed to estimate and suggest the type of fertilizer to be applied to crops or plants.
